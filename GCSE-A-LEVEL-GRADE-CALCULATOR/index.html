How To Use the Grade Calculator

Enter the total marks for your exam and the marks you achieved. The calculator shows your percentage and translates it to GCSE and A-Level grades.

Why percentages matter

Percentages make it easy to compare performance across different exams and boards, ensuring grades are consistent.

Exam grading scales

GCSE grades run from 9 to 1, while A-Level grades use A* to U. The thresholds used here are common benchmarks and can be adjusted by your school or board.
