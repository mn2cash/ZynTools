FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about this Petrol Price Tracker

How does this tool work?

The tracker fetches fuel price averages from a demo JSON mirror, then calculates day-on-day and week-on-week deltas and plots them on the history chart.

Is this tool free?

Yes, this demo tracker is free to use and requires no signup.

How often does the data update?

In production you can point the fetch URL to an hourly or daily feed; the demo data refreshes every page load.

Is this accurate?

The demo data is illustrative. For accuracy, connect to a trusted UK fuel price feed or CSV mirror.
