How to use the BMI tool

Enter your height in centimeters and weight in kilograms, then tap calculate. Your BMI, category, and a healthy weight range for your height will appear instantly.

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index is a quick screening metric comparing weight to height. It helps gauge if you are underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese based on population studies.

BMI categories

The scale highlights four bands: Underweight (below 18.5), Healthy (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), and Obese (30+). Your marker shows exactly where you land.

Frequently asked questions

How does this tool work?

The calculator divides your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared, then places the result on the BMI scale with an instant category.

Is this tool free?

Yes, the BMI calculator is free to use and optimized for mobile and desktop devices.

How often does the data update?

Results update immediately whenever you change your height or weight and press calculate or hit Enter.

Is this accurate?

BMI is a general guideline and does not account for muscle mass or medical conditions. For personalized advice, consult a healthcare professional.
