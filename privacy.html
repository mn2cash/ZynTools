Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ZynTools handles information in a GDPR-friendly, minimal way. We design our tools to use as little personal data as possible.

Data we collect

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, generic analytics).
  • Non-personal technical data (browser type, approximate region).

Cookies & AdSense

We use cookies to support Google AdSense and basic analytics. AdSense may set cookies to deliver and measure ads. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

Third-party disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Limited data may be processed by analytics or advertising providers solely to deliver services.

User rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a summary of data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete identifiable data where applicable.
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate information.
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential tracking where offered.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for functionality, security, or legal compliance, then remove or anonymize it.

Contact

Email us at privacy@yourdomain.com for privacy requests.
